Data Types in Python
--------------------------------------------

Operators
- Arithmetic Operators
  +, -, /, *, //, %, **
- Assignment Operators
  =, +=, -=, /=, *=, %=
- Comparison Operators
  >, <, ==, !=, >=, <=
- Logical Operators
  and, or, not
- Membership Operator
  in, not in
- Identity Operators
  is, is not
- Bitwise Operator
  &, |, ^
